Ingredients: 20 cherry tomatoes 20 small fresh mozzarella balls (bocconcini) 20 fresh basil leaves Balsamic glaze for drizzling Salt and pepper to taste Wooden skewers or toothpicks Instructions: Prepare the Skewers: a. Soak wooden skewers in water for about 30 minutes to prevent them from burning while grilling or broiling. Alternatively, you can use toothpicks if you prefer bite-sized skewers. Assemble the Skewers: a. Take a cherry tomato and slide it onto the skewer or toothpick. b. Follow with a fresh basil leaf folded in half. c. Add a mozzarella ball. d. Repeat this pattern with another cherry tomato, basil leaf, and mozzarella ball. e. Continue assembling the skewers until all the ingredients are used. Season and Drizzle: a. Place the assembled skewers on a serving platter or plate. b. Season the skewers with a sprinkle of salt and pepper to taste. c. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the skewers, ensuring each skewer gets a light coating. You can also serve the glaze on the side for individual drizzling. Serve: a. Serve the Caprese skewers immediately or refrigerate them until ready to serve. b. If refrigerated, take them out about 10-15 minutes before serving to allow them to come to room temperature and enhance the flavors. c. Arrange the skewers on a platter or serving dish, and they are ready to be enjoyed! Tip: You can also enhance the presentation by serving the skewers on a bed of fresh arugula or drizzling with extra virgin olive oil before adding the balsamic glaze. Caprese skewers make a delightful and elegant appetizer or finger food for parties, gatherings, or any occasion. Enjoy!
